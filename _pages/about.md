## Purpose

This is for everyone who:

- Serves the people
- Takes pride in their part
- Believes there is always a better way
- Does the hard work to deliver it

## How it's organized

Each chapter has these sections:

* Overview
* The problem
* The solution
* Context
* Do’s and don’ts
* Checklist
* Mantras
* Questions to ask
* Learn more
